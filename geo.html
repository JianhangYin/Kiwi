RESEARCH

The research I was focused on is aiming to understand the subsurface small-scale structure such as faults, karst and pinch-outs, which is tightly related to the oil exploration. By applying different methods to the seismic data, I extract and image diffraction to obtain diffractor images with high resolution. Three methods I introduced are listed as follows:

  • Kernel Principle Component Analysis (KPCA)
  • Geometric-mean Reverse-time Migration (GmRTM)
  • Common Reflection Surface (CRS)
